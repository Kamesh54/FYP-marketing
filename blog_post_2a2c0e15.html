The Story Behind Every Grain

Chennai breathes biriyani. Yet amid the crowded lanes and cloud kitchens, Flavour & Spices dares to ask: what if every grain told a story of terroir, tradition, and technology?

Our journey started with a simple observation—while Chennai’s appetite for biriyani is insatiable, most outlets settle for shortcuts: pre-mixed powders, par-boiled rice, and generic gravies. We chose the longer route.

Great biriyani is not assembled; it is cultivated—seed to spice, farm to fork.

Hyper-Local Spice Palette

We work directly with farmers in Karaikudi for kalpasi (black stone flower), Ramanathapuram for hand-pounded seeraga samba rice, and the Western Ghats for aromatic maratti mokku (dried flower pods). These micro-ingredients rarely appear on commercial invoices, yet they define the unique value proposition of our brand.

Signature Garam

Our house garam is toasted at 60 °C for exactly 4 min 30 sec to preserve volatile terpenes, then stone-ground in small batches. The result: a layered heat that blooms minutes after you swallow, not seconds.

Time as an Ingredient

We borrowed the dum technique from Nawabi kitchens and paired it with sous-vide precision. Each handi is sealed with a veil of dough and slow-cooked at 92 °C for 2.5 hours. This low-temp hold prevents starch rupture, yielding rice that stays al dente even after 45 min of delivery.

Zero-Waste Broth

Vegetable trimmings and herb stems are pressure-cycled into a robust broth that substitutes plain water while cooking rice—cutting kitchen waste by 38 %.

Cloud Kitchen, Clear Conscience

Located in OMR, our ghost kitchen operates on a single-app model—orders, inventory, and rider hand-off synced through one tablet. No dine-in footprint means lower operational complexity and 22 % faster order turnaround versus brick-and-mortar outlets.

Since 2024, we have served 12,000+ plates with a 4.8/5 rating across Swiggy & Zomato, achieving a 1 M run rate within 10 months—proof that low-risk, high-efficiency cloud kitchens can still deliver craft-quality food.
